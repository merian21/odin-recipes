Chickpea Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • cashews
  • zucchini
  • 1 pack of cut tomatoes
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • spices

Steps

  1. cook rice as advised on packaging
  2. roast cashews in pan
  3. dry off chickpeas
  4. add chickpeas to pan
  5. add zucchini to pan
  6. wait for everything to fr a bit
  7. add coconut milk and tomatoes
  8. add spice and serve with rice

    9. done :)
