Mie Noodles

Ingredients

for pan

  • 250g mie noodles
  • 2 tblspns sesame oil
  • 400g asia veggiemix
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 tblspn fresh ginger
  • 1 tblspn sesame
  • spring onion
  • salt & pepper

for peanut sauce

  • 8 tblspns soy sauce
  • 4 tblspns brown sugar
  • 3 tblspns rice vinegar
  • 2 tblspns peanut mousse

Steps

  1. cook noodles as advised on packaging
  2. get rid of noodle water and put noodles aside
  3. mix ingredients for peanut sauce
  4. heat sesame oil in a big pan and fry veggies for 5 minutes
  5. add ginger and garlic, fry for 3 more minutes
  6. add noodles and sesame to pan, stir and spice with salt & pepper
  7. put into bowls and add spring onion on top

    8. done :)
